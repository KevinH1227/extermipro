About Us

"Your home is our priority."

"Your home is our priority", is not only our slogan but the foundation of our pest control mission, dedicated to safeguarding your comfort and safety."

At Extermi-Pro, we make it a point to always go the extra mile for our clients. Whether it's during our monthly inspections, a treatment for a single residential unit, or a large-scale commercial building, we always apply the same rigorous approach. In short, we never cut corners. Our head technician is also a co-owner of the company, ensuring that all work, whether big or small, is carried out meticulously according to a pre-established procedure with the client. The client/technician cooperation is paramount, and we always take the time to ask the right questions to precisely understand the issue at hand. As with any project, preparation is the most crucial step. For certain treatments, there is a strict protocol that must be followed. We offer all the necessary assistance, such as cleaning, pressure washing, or repairing holes or access points (for mice or wasps, etc.), to maximize the chances of a successful treatment. All our work is guaranteed (unless specified otherwise). Feel free to give us a call or send us a message. Trying us is adopting us!
